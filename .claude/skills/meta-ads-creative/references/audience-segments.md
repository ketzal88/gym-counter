## Segment Details

### New-to-Homeschool

**Who They Are:**
- Parents considering homeschool for first time
- Often triggered by negative school experience
- Researching options, feeling overwhelmed

**Key Objections:**
- "Am I qualified to teach?"
- "Where do I even start?"
- "What curriculum do I use?"

**Winning Messages:**
- "You don't need a teaching degree"
- "Support and guidance included"
- "Everything you need in one place"

---

### Budget-Conscious

**Who They Are:**
- Want to homeschool but worried about cost
- May be single-income families
- Often surprised by curriculum prices

**Key Objections:**
- "We can't afford it"
- "Free programs must be lower quality"
- "Hidden costs?"

**Winning Messages:**
- "Tuition-free through public school partnerships"
- "Premium curriculum included"
- "No hidden costs, no catches"

---

### Burned-Out Public School

**Who They Are:**
- Kids struggling or unhappy in school
- Parents frustrated with system
- Often have tried interventions that failed

**Key Objections:**
- "Will homeschool be different?"
- "What if my kid still struggles?"
- "Is this running away from problems?"

**Winning Messages:**
- "Different environment, different results"
- "When education fits, kids thrive"
- "It's not running away, it's running toward"

---

### Experienced Homeschooler

**Who They Are:**
- Already homeschooling 1+ years
- Looking for better resources or support
- May be burned out on doing it alone

**Key Objections:**
- "I already have what I need"
- "Will this work with my approach?"
- "Too much oversight?"

**Winning Messages:**
- "Maintain your freedom, add support"
- "Access resources without changing your style"
- "Support when you want it"

---

### Special Needs Parent

**Who They Are:**
- Child has learning differences, neurodivergence, or disabilities
- Traditional school not meeting needs
- Often spent years fighting for accommodations

**Key Objections:**
- "Can you handle my child's needs?"
- "Do you have specialized support?"
- "Will my child get worse without professionals?"

**Winning Messages:**
- "Truly personalized learning"
- "Go at your child's pace"
- "Support that adapts to your child"

---

### Working Parent

**Who They Are:**
- Both parents work or single working parent
- Worried about time and flexibility
- May need childcare component

**Key Objections:**
- "I don't have time to homeschool"
- "How does this work with work schedules?"
- "Who teaches while I'm working?"

**Winning Messages:**
- "Flexible scheduling that works around you"
- "3 focused hours vs 8 school hours"
- "Options for every family structure"
