# Ad Formats Library

**Purpose:** Reference guide to all ad formats with structure templates, best uses, and examples.

---

## Format Categories

1. **Lo-Fi Native Formats** - Feel like organic content
2. **Social Proof Formats** - Leverage existing engagement
3. **UGC Formats** - Real people, authentic feel
4. **Text-Over-Video Formats** - Story through text sequences
5. **Meme Formats** - Cultural reference patterns
6. **Testimonial Formats** - Direct social proof
7. **Carousel Formats** - Multi-slide storytelling
8. **Static Image Formats** - Simple, bold messaging

---

## LO-FI NATIVE FORMATS

### Notes App Format

**Description:** Screenshot of iPhone Notes app with list or checklist

**Why It Works:**
- Looks like personal content, not an ad
- Familiar format everyone recognizes
- Scannable, easy to consume
- Native to the platform

**Structure Template:**
```
[TITLE IN CAPS]

[Setup/Problem list]
- Item 1
- Item 2
- Item 3

[Transition/Pivot]

[Solution/Result list]
- Item 1
- Item 2
- Item 3

[Tag line or CTA]
```

**Best For:**
- Problem-solution contrast
- Before/after comparisons
- Checklists and "starter packs"
- Relatable struggles

**Example Hooks:**
- "HOMESCHOOL MOM STARTER PACK:"
- "WHAT I EXPECTED VS WHAT HAPPENED:"
- "THINGS SCHOOLS DON'T TELL YOU:"
- "MY BUDGET BEFORE VS AFTER OPENED:"

---

### Handwritten/Sticky Note Format

**Description:** Text that looks handwritten or on a sticky note

**Why It Works:**
- Personal, intimate feel
- Stands out from polished content
- Triggers "found content" curiosity
- Simple, direct messaging

**Structure Template:**
```
[Short, punchy message]

[Optional second line]

- [signature or attribution]
```

**Best For:**
- Simple, powerful statements
- Personal confessions
- Quick tips or insights
- Emotional messages

**Example Hooks:**
- "I wish I knew this before..."
- "Note to self:"
- "Reminder:"
- "The thing nobody tells you:"

---

### Screenshot/Phone Recording Format

**Description:** Looks like screenshot from phone with UI elements

**Why It Works:**
- Feels like peer sharing discovery
- Authentic, not produced
- "I found this thing" energy
- Native to mobile experience

**Structure Template:**
```
[Phone UI elements - time, battery, etc.]

[Content being shared]

[Reaction/caption below]
```

**Best For:**
- Sharing resources or discoveries
- "Check this out" energy
- Data or statistics
- Quick testimonials

## SOCIAL PROOF FORMATS

### Reddit Post Format

**Description:** Styled to look like a Reddit post with upvotes/comments

**Why It Works:**
- Discovery/finding energy
- Community validation (upvotes)
- Authentic voice expectations
- "Real person" feel

**Structure Template:**
```
r/[subreddit]

[Post Title - usually a question or statement]

[Body text - casual, conversational tone]

[Upvote count] | [Comment count]
```

**Best For:**
- Personal stories and experiences
- Questions and discoveries
- Community-style advice
- "I found this" moments

**Subreddits to Reference:**
- r/homeschool
- r/Parenting
- r/specialneeds
- r/education

---

### Tweet/X Screenshot Format

**Description:** Styled to look like a tweet with engagement metrics

**Why It Works:**
- Viral/shareable energy
- Punchy, concise messaging
- Social proof via likes/retweets
- Familiar format

**Structure Template:**
```
@[username]

[Tweet text - max 280 chars ideal]

[Engagement: Likes | Retweets | Comments]
```

**Best For:**
- Hot takes and opinions
- Personal revelations
- Quick tips
- Viral-style content

**Example Hooks:**
- "Wild story:"
- "PSA for parents:"
- "This changed everything:"
- "Hot take:"

---

### Instagram Comment Format

**Description:** Fake Instagram comment with video response

**Why It Works:**
- Creates dialogue/conversation feel
- "Answering your question" energy
- Relatable question/concern
- Natural CTA to learn more

**Structure Template:**
```
[Username]: [Comment/question]

[Video response answering the comment]
```

**Best For:**
- Addressing objections
- FAQ-style content
- Personal responses
- "Yes, and here's how" content

---

### Group Post Screenshot

**Description:** Looks like screenshot from Facebook group

**Why It Works:**
- Community recommendation feel
- "Someone like me found this"
- Discovery energy
- Authentic endorsement

**Structure Template:**
```
[Group Name]

[Post text with personal story/recommendation]

[Reactions and comments preview]
```

**Best For:**
- Recommendations
- Personal testimonials
- "I was skeptical but..."
- Success stories

## UGC FORMATS

### Talking Head (Phone Selfie)

**Description:** Person talking directly to camera, iPhone footage feel

**Why It Works:**
- Human connection and trust
- Authentic, not produced
- Personal story delivery
- Eye contact creates engagement

**Structure Template:**
```
[HOOK - First 3 seconds, bold claim or question]

[SETUP - Quick context/backstory]

[TRANSFORMATION - What changed]

[PROOF - Specific results or outcomes]

[CTA - What to do next]
```

**Best For:**
- Personal testimonials
- Founder/expert stories
- Emotional transformations
- Trust-building

**Filming Tips:**
- Natural lighting preferred
- Authentic setting (kitchen, car, office)
- Eye level camera angle
- Conversational tone

---

### Car Confession Format

**Description:** Person talking to camera while in parked car

**Why It Works:**
- "Just had to share this" energy
- Private, confessional feel
- Relatable setting
- Authentic moment capture

**Structure Template:**
```
[In car, usually parked]

"Okay I have to tell you something..."

[Story or revelation]

"I just had to share this"
```

**Best For:**
- Emotional stories
- Recent discoveries
- Personal revelations
- "I can't believe this" moments

---

### "Get Ready With Me" Format

**Description:** Person doing routine while explaining something

**Why It Works:**
- Multi-tasking relatable
- Feels like conversation, not pitch
- Long-form attention holding
- Authentic daily life

**Structure Template:**
```
[Person doing routine - makeup, coffee, etc.]

"So while I'm getting ready, let me tell you about..."

[Story or explanation throughout]
```

**Best For:**
- Longer explanations
- Background stories
- Casual information sharing
- Building connection

---

### B-Roll with Voiceover

**Description:** Lifestyle footage with voice narrating story

**Why It Works:**
- Cinematic but accessible
- Story without seeing speaker
- Flexible with footage
- Emotional impact

**Structure Template:**
```
[B-roll of relevant activity]

[Voiceover telling story]

[Text overlays for emphasis]
```

**Best For:**
- Emotional stories
- Family/lifestyle content
- Product/service in use
- Aspirational outcomes

## TEXT-OVER-VIDEO FORMATS

### Quick-Cut Text Sequence

**Description:** Fast-paced text appearing with B-roll cuts

**Why It Works:**
- Holds attention with pace
- Works without sound
- Digestible in scroll
- Punchy messaging

**Structure Template:**
```
[Text 1 - Hook/problem] - 1-2 seconds
[Text 2 - Agitate/context] - 1-2 seconds
[Text 3 - Solution hint] - 1-2 seconds
[Text 4 - Reveal] - 2-3 seconds
[Text 5 - CTA] - 2-3 seconds
```

**Best For:**
- Problem-solution arcs
- Quick tips
- Before/after stories
- Punchy messaging

---

### "This Is Your Sign" Format

**Description:** Text sequence building to a reveal

**Why It Works:**
- Familiar format
- Creates permission to act
- Builds anticipation
- Feels like personal message

**Structure Template:**
```
"This is your sign..."

[Pain point or desire they're feeling]

[Validation]

[Solution reveal]

[Product/brand as answer]

[CTA]
```

**Best For:**
- Permission-giving messaging
- Addressing hesitation
- Aspirational outcomes
- Emotional connection

---

### "POV" Format

**Description:** Point-of-view future visualization

**Why It Works:**
- Creates desire for outcome
- Future-pacing technique
- Personal and specific
- Aspirational

**Structure Template:**
```
"POV: It's [future date]"

[Desired outcome achieved]

[How they got there]

[Your product/service as the key]

[CTA to start now]
```

**Best For:**
- Outcome visualization
- Long-term benefits
- Aspirational messaging
- Decision motivation

---

### "Nobody Talks About" Format

**Description:** Revealing hidden truth or insight

**Why It Works:**
- Creates curiosity gap
- Insider information feel
- Authority positioning
- Share-worthy content

**Structure Template:**
```
"Nobody talks about [hidden thing]"

[The insight or truth]

[Why it matters]

[What to do about it]
```

**Best For:**
- Industry insights
- Counterintuitive truths
- Expert positioning
- Educational content

## MEME FORMATS

### Drake Approval Meme

**Description:** Two-panel format showing rejection/approval

**Why It Works:**
- Instantly recognizable
- Clear contrast communication
- Humorous tone
- Highly shareable

**Structure Template:**
```
[Top panel - Drake disapproves]
[Thing to avoid/reject]

[Bottom panel - Drake approves]
[Thing to embrace/do instead]
```

**Best For:**
- Simple contrasts
- Behavior comparison
- Feature highlights
- Lighthearted messaging

---

### Expectation vs Reality

**Description:** Two-panel showing contrast between expectation and reality

**Why It Works:**
- Relatable humor
- Addresses fears
- Reality can be positive or negative
- Share-worthy format

**Structure Template:**
```
EXPECTATION:
[What people think will happen]

REALITY:
[What actually happens]
```

**Best For:**
- Fear addressing
- Positive surprises
- Relatable struggles
- Humor-driven messaging

---

### "Nobody: / Me:" Format

**Description:** Setup with "Nobody:" then unexpected action

**Why It Works:**
- Highlights relatable behavior
- Self-deprecating humor
- Community building
- Highly shareable

**Structure Template:**
```
Nobody:

[Entity]: "[Relatable quote or action]"

Me: *[Relatable reaction or action]*
```

**Best For:**
- Relatable behaviors
- Community humor
- Self-aware messaging
- Lighthearted content

---

### Distracted Boyfriend

**Description:** Three-panel with distraction from current to new

**Why It Works:**
- Universal recognition
- Clear narrative
- Relationship dynamic
- Attention metaphor

**Structure Template:**
```
[Girlfriend - thing being left behind]
[Other woman - new attractive thing]
[Boyfriend - the person making choice]
```

**Best For:**
- Switching behavior
- New discovery
- Leaving old ways
- Product comparison

---

### This vs That Comparison

**Description:** Side-by-side comparison format

**Why It Works:**
- Clear differentiation
- Easy to scan
- Logical appeal
- Decision support

**Structure Template:**
```
[OPTION A]          [OPTION B]

Feature 1: X        Feature 1: Y
Feature 2: X        Feature 2: Y
Feature 3: X        Feature 3: Y
```

**Best For:**
- Product comparison
- Before/after
- Old way vs new way
- Feature highlights

## TESTIMONIAL FORMATS

### Quote Card

**Description:** Quote with attribution on designed background

**Why It Works:**
- Social proof centered
- Scannable
- Specific and credible
- Shareable

**Structure Template:**
```
"[Direct quote - specific outcome or transformation]"

- [Name], OpenEd Parent
```

**Best For:**
- Specific outcomes
- Emotional stories
- Credibility building
- Warm audiences

**Design Tips:**
- Clean background
- Readable font
- Optional photo
- Brand colors subtle

---

### Video Testimonial

**Description:** Real customer speaking to camera

**Why It Works:**
- Highest credibility
- Emotional connection
- Authentic delivery
- Trust building

**Structure Template:**
```
[INTRO - Who they are, context]
[PROBLEM - What they were facing]
[DISCOVERY - How they found the solution]
[RESULT - Specific outcomes]
[RECOMMENDATION - What they'd tell others]
```

**Best For:**
- Warm/retargeted audiences
- High-consideration decisions
- Trust building
- Specific segments

---

### Montage Testimonial

**Description:** Multiple quick clips from different customers

**Why It Works:**
- Volume of proof
- Variety of perspectives
- Keeps attention with cuts
- Broad appeal

**Structure Template:**
```
[Person 1 - 3-5 second clip]
[Person 2 - 3-5 second clip]
[Person 3 - 3-5 second clip]
[Summary/CTA]
```

**Best For:**
- Broad claims
- Community feel
- Addressing multiple objections
- "Everyone loves it" energy

---

## CAROUSEL FORMATS

### Story Arc Carousel

**Description:** Multiple slides telling a story with progression

**Why It Works:**
- Engagement through swipes
- Full story retention
- Each slide hooks next
- Higher time on ad

**Structure Template:**
```
Slide 1: Hook/Title
Slide 2: Problem/Setup
Slide 3: Turning point
Slide 4: Solution/Discovery
Slide 5: Outcome/Proof
Slide 6: CTA
```

**Best For:**
- Complex stories
- Educational content
- Before/after journeys
- Feature explanations

---

### List Carousel

**Description:** Each slide is one item in a list

**Why It Works:**
- Clear progression
- Easy to consume
- Shareable individual slides
- Complete information

**Structure Template:**
```
Slide 1: "[Number] Ways to [Outcome]"
Slide 2-N: One way per slide
Final Slide: CTA
```

**Best For:**
- Tips and advice
- Feature lists
- Reasons to buy
- Educational content

---

### Before/After Carousel

**Description:** Alternating before and after slides

**Why It Works:**
- Visual transformation
- Story through contrast
- Multiple examples possible
- Proof of concept

**Structure Template:**
```
Slide 1: "Before & After [Topic]"
Slide 2: Before Story 1
Slide 3: After Story 1
Slide 4: Before Story 2
Slide 5: After Story 2
Final Slide: CTA
```

**Best For:**
- Transformation stories
- Multiple testimonials
- Results showcase
- Proof stacking

---

## STATIC IMAGE FORMATS

### Bold Statement

**Description:** Simple text on solid or gradient background

**Why It Works:**
- Stops scroll with words
- Clear message
- No distraction
- High impact

**Structure Template:**
```
[Bold, short statement]

[Optional second line]

[Brand/CTA small]
```

**Best For:**
- Strong positioning
- Thought leadership
- Awareness campaigns
- Statement pieces

---

### Statistics/Numbers

**Description:** Key stats or numbers prominently displayed

**Why It Works:**
- Specific = credible
- Quick to process
- Shareable data
- Logical appeal

**Structure Template:**
```
[BIG NUMBER]

[What it means]

[Brand attribution]
```

**Best For:**
- Social proof
- Results claims
- Urgency/scarcity
- Credibility building

---

### Product/Feature Highlight

**Description:** Simple image highlighting specific feature

**Why It Works:**
- Clear communication
- Easy to understand
- Works in feed
- Direct messaging

**Structure Template:**
```
[Visual of feature/benefit]

[Short explanatory text]

[CTA]
```

**Best For:**
- Feature launches
- Specific benefits
- Simple offers
- Clear value props

## FORMAT SELECTION GUIDE

### By Audience Temperature

| Audience | Best Formats |
|----------|--------------|
| Cold | Notes App, Meme, Text-Over-Video, Reddit/Tweet |
| Warm | UGC, Testimonial, Carousel, Before/After |
| Hot | Direct Testimonial, Feature Highlight, Statistics |

### By Objective

| Objective | Best Formats |
|-----------|--------------|
| Awareness | Meme, Bold Statement, Text-Over-Video |
| Consideration | UGC, Carousel, Before/After |
| Conversion | Testimonial, Statistics, Feature Highlight |

### By Message Type

| Message | Best Formats |
|---------|--------------|
| Problem-Solution | Notes App, Text-Over-Video, Carousel |
| Social Proof | Reddit/Tweet, Testimonial, Statistics |
| Transformation | Before/After, UGC, POV Format |
| Education | Carousel, "Nobody Talks About", Talking Head |
| Urgency | Bold Statement, Statistics, Direct Offer |
